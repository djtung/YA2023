You follow the butler's instructions and make your way up the grand staircase to the second floor. As you walk through the halls, Emma can't help but marvel at the opulence of the place. You reach the master bedroom and open the door, immediately struck by the grandeur of the room. The ceiling was high and adorned with frescoes, the walls were covered in silk tapestries, and the floors were made of dark hardwood.
Inside the room you see a very regular looking table that doesn't match the decor of the room at all. On it lay simple plastic cups with small ping pong balls. "This might be something", Emma remarks. Ask the game masters about Cups.
