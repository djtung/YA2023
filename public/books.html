The Bookshelf

As you bounce the last ball into the cup, you notice the wall adjacent start to shift. The wall which was previously covered with a tasteful wallpaper turns to reveal a large bookshelf. "That's new", Emma says. You approach and get a closer look at the large array of old books which lined the shelves. One book stands out among the rest, you pull it out, the dust shaking off the book as you grip it.
"It's a Bible", Emma notes correctly. "NIV version." Inside the cover on the first page you can see the following verses on a note: Genesis 9:11 - 7 Matthew 1:21 - 3 2 Chronicles 14:7 - 4 Hebrews 10:36 - 4 Deuteronomy 11:1 - 17
