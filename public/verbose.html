The Club

You enter the word 'constructiveness' into the keypad. Suddenly, the entire room comes to life with a dull hum. Lights which line the door flicker on and illuminate several buttons on the door which were previously imperceptible. You push the buttons and the door croaks open. Behind the door, you find an old storage closet with dusty shelves. Various janitorial tools which seemed as old as Emma was lined the shelves. Near the back of the room, you see a tall workbench with various papers scattered across it. As you look through the papers, you find a clue that describes a secret passage in another place called "Borealis". "Any idea what this is?", you ask Emma. "There is a famous bar here in the city named Borealis, I'm sure it is referring to that". You quickly make your way over. You and Emma approach the door but the bouncer stops you. "Back of the line", he says angrily. Despite your best efforts to convince him of the importance of your quest, he doesn't seem to want to let you in.
You persist. "Tell you what, if you play my game I'll let you in. But if you can't win, I don't want to see you here again", he says with a smirk. You agree and he takes out a box behind him. Ask the game masters about Words.
