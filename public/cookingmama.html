The Kitchen

"Impressive, you can come in", he says to you as he holds the door open. You give Emma a smile as you make your way inside Borealis, to the dismay of the other patrons waiting in the long line. As you enter, you are greeted with the sound of lively music and the smell of exotic cocktails. The bar was crowded, with people dancing and having a good time. You make your way through the crowd, searching for the next clue. You try to find the secret passage mentioned in the notes you found previously, and see a door at the end of a hallway near the back which looks like it could be it. As you enter the door, you see a host of busy workers preparing dishes. It's the kitchen. The kitchen is bustling as the chefs work rapidly on the tickets, too busy to even notice you had entered. As you turn to leave, you hear the head chef ask, "Are you the new hire?".
Before you can even reply, he continues on. "Never mind, just get to the office and help us organize the new recipes". You don't want to cause a scene so you do as he says. As you enter the office, you find the recipes on the computer, but they are unnamed. "This shouldn't take long", Emma says. "Let's try to identify them based on the steps."
