The Pieces

You tap the morse code from the radio directly into the telegraph sounder as you hear it. Suddenly, an intricate door near the side of the room slides open to reveal a long, narrow room. The room was dimly lit, but you could see that the walls were lined with ancient texts and scrolls, some of which were in languages that couldn't be recognized. Even the lightest footsteps lift plumes of dust from the scrolls and texts around you. You and Emma cover your face, as the previously stagnant air becomes thick with dusty particles. As you make your way deeper into the room, you stumble upon a strange looking mechanism on a raised tablet. Beside it, small stone pieces which seem to depict some image if they could be arranged correctly.
"The space on the tablet looks just big enough for these pieces", Emma notes. Ask the game masters about Jigsaw Puzzles.
