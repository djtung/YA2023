The Letter

Emma Williams was a 28-year-old woman who had always felt like she was meant for something greater. She had grown up in a small town, and had always dreamed of traveling the world and having adventures. But after finishing college, she had settled for a menial office job that left her feeling unfulfilled and stuck in a rut. Recently, Emma inherited her uncle's house who passed away recently and decided to move in to start anew. As she was going through the boxes of old possessions in her attic, she came across a yellowed envelope that caught her eye. The letter inside was from her long lost grandfather Walt, whom she had not seen since she was a child. The letter spoke of an ancient familial treasure buried somewhere in the world and his attempts to find it, but to no avail. She had heard of this treasure in passing from family members before, but everyone always passed it off as rumors and fairy tales. Now, she had the confirmation she needed. As she read through the letter, Emma felt a sense of excitement and anticipation. But she also knew that it would be impossible to complete the hunt by herself, especially with her grandfather already trying without success. And with that, she decided to visit a private investigator to help her in this journey.
After doing some research, she decides to enlist the world famous investigators from CWC to help her. She finds them at _____ Watt Rd, Lindell Beach, BC.
