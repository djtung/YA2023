The Tunnel

You enter 'envelope' into the console and at first nothing seems to happen. Suddenly, you hear a faint rumbling which grows louder and louder as the stone in front of you rolls over, exposing a dark tunnel. You step into the tunnel, Emma following in tow. The walls were made of rough-hewn stone. You and Emma can feel a chill in the air as you walk through it. Finally, as you reach the end, you see a small table carved in the middle of a small room. On it is a marked chess board, though some of the pieces are missing. In the corner of the room, there is a large combination lock.
On the table next to the chess board is a small piece of paper with instructions, barely legible due to the age of the note. "White to move. Move the piece to the square which checkmates the black king." "Enter the piece followed by the square coordinate. For example, moving the Rook to F4 would be 'Rf4'." "B = Bishop. R = Rook. N = Knight. Q = Queen."
