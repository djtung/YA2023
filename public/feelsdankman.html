The Third Victory!

It takes you and Emma together to open the heavy lid of the chest. Inside was a glittering array of treasure, the chest filled to the brim with gold and precious gems. Also inside were intricate carvings of animals and ancient relics from a forgotten time. They looked valuable. Emma let out a gasp of amazement as she laid her eyes on the treasure. The fact that you had barely survived your encounter in the rainforest makes the experience even sweeter.
At the bottom of the chest you spot a sealed envelope. Inside, a letter with the words Departure: Vancouver was highlighted on it. It seems important so you keep the letter with your personal belongings. You and Emma share a hug and begin to make your way back to the city, eager for the next leg of your journey. Return to the airport.
